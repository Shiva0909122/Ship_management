Crew Management

AD Ship Management offers complete crew management and recruitment services for all types of vessels. Our highly designated, skilled and experienced personnel provide efficient and personalized services to our clients by maintaining a regular communication with clients, authorities. Hence, it also helps us to excel in customer satisfaction.

We offer Crew management and Crew supply services with flexible & tailored solutions to meet client specific needs. We focus on qualitative, quantitative and effective process in our crew selection with proper weight-age in regards to the past experience, technical interview and the assessment of every crew members as per requirements, principles required for mandatory certification.

Quality is the prime focus and we ensure that you receive the best from our end. Our determination rests in the trust and belief that we have in our seafarers so as to support and expand their career and growth with safety, which in return helps us in our growth and prosperity.
